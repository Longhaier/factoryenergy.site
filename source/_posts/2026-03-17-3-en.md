# HVAC Optimization: Going from 80 to 95 Points

## The 80-Point HVAC System

Honestly, most factory HVAC systems are just "getting by."

They cool, they heat, temperatures aren't too extreme, air quality's acceptable—good enough, around 80 points.

But have you ever thought about how much you could save by going from 80 to 95 points?

**Our factory's real numbers: 400,000+ RMB saved per year.**

## What's a 95-Point HVAC?

Simply put: more stable temperatures, lower energy consumption, better comfort.

Specific metrics:
- Indoor temp fluctuation within ±1℃ (not ±3℃)
- Energy efficiency improved 15%+
- Equipment lifespan extended 30%
- Maintenance costs cut 50%

## How to Get There

### 1. Control System Optimization (Most Critical)

This is where you get **maximum results with minimum investment**.

**Setpoints must be reasonable:**
- Cooling setpoint 26℃ in summer (not 24℃—1℃ = 7% energy savings)
- Heating setpoint 20℃ in winter
- Flexible pre-cooling/pre-heating schedules

**Control strategies must be smart:**
- Adjust setpoints based on outdoor temperature
- Zone control instead of "one size fits all"
- Automatic energy-saving mode at night/weekends

When we retrofitted our control system with remote monitoring and smart algorithms, **we saved 120,000 kWh in one summer alone**.

### 2. Proper Equipment Maintenance

**Filter cleaning:**
- Clean primary/secondary filters monthly
- Clogged filters increase energy consumption by 20%

**Condenser/evaporator cleaning:**
- 0.3mm fouling drops efficiency by 15%
- Clean at least twice yearly

**Refrigerant management:**
- Regular leak checks
- Correct charge—neither too much nor too little

### 3. Optimize Run Times

- Shut down main units 15 minutes early (residual cooling/heating is enough)
- Enter energy-saving mode when people leave
- Automatic rotation on rest days/holidays

### 4. Energy Monitoring

Install power meters with sub-metering:
- Main unit consumption
- Fan consumption
- Pump consumption

Analyze monthly—investigate anomalies immediately.
