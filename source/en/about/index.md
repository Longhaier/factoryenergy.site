# About Factory Energy Management

Factory Energy Management (factoryenergy.site) is a professional knowledge platform dedicated to energy efficiency and facility management in semiconductor manufacturing plants.

## Our Mission

We provide the latest insights, best practices, and case studies on:
- Compressed air systems optimization
- Cleanroom HVAC management
- Power system efficiency
- Predictive maintenance
- Water and energy conservation
